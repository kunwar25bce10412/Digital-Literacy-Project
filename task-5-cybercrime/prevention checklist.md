# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

## General Safety Tips
- Use strong and unique passwords for all your accounts.
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible.
- Do not share OTPs, passwords, or personal details with anyone.
- Always verify links before clicking, especially from unknown sources.
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for sensitive activities like banking.

## UPI & Financial Safety
- Never approve unknown payment requests on UPI apps.
- Do not scan QR codes sent by strangers or unknown contacts.
- Regularly check your bank statements for suspicious transactions.
- Install only official banking and payment apps from trusted sources.

## Online Behavior & Awareness
- Keep your social media accounts private and limit personal information.
- Do not download files or apps from untrusted websites.
- Be cautious of messages that create urgency (e.g., “your account will be blocked”).
- Log out from shared or public devices after use.

## Reporting Cybercrime
- Report incidents immediately at: https://cybercrime.gov.in
- Call the Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 (24x7)
