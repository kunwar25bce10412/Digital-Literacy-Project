# Social Media Do’s and Don’ts 

## Do’s
- Make sure your content reflects your personality positively.
- Use a decent photo and appropriate bio.
- Avoid spreading fake news.
- Be polite and avoid offensive language.
- Control who can see your posts.

## Don’ts
- Avoid posting phone number, address, etc.
- You may regret it later.
- It harms others and your reputation.
- It can risk your privacy.
- They may be scams or phishing attempts.
